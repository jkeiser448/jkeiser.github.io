For the print project, I had to create a bookmark for a local elementary school. I started by sketching out 5 designs, and narrowed it down to one. With that one design, I created it on InDesign. Then I added it to a file with my other group members.

Once we had our five bookmarks together, we made our print plate. To make the final book marks we learned how to run the press using our plate.
