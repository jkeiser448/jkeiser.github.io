For the video project, we had to make a one minute video about something involving Millersville. My group and I are all members of TEECA, Technology and Engineering Education Collegiate Association, at Millersville. So, we decided to make a video about the TEECA East Competition we attended.

At the competition we all took part in different challenges such as robotics, communications, manufacturing, and problem solving to name a few. In between these challenges we got to attend a banquet with a keynote speaker, compete in a trivia game, and have fun at the boardwalk. We tried our best to showcase everything that went on with the one minute we had.
