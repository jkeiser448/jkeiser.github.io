For the photography project, we had the option to go out and take photos, or find existing photos that we could edit. We used photoshops to crop our images and remove objects. We also used photoshop to change the colors, highlights, exposure, and other adjustments. I had a lot of fun with this project because it taught me skills that I can use any time I want to make improvements to photos I take.
